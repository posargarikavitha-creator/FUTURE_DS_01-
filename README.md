# FUTURE_DS_01 – Business Sales Performance Analytics

## 📌 Task Overview
This project is Task 1 of the Data Science & Analytics Internship at Future Interns.
The goal is to analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling
products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop

## 📊 Dataset
Global Superstore Dataset (sourced from Kaggle)
Columns: Order ID, Order Date, Customer Name, Segment, Region, Category,
Sub-Category, Product Name, Sales, Quantity, Discount, Profit, etc.

## 🔑 Key Insights
1. Sales remained stable with a slight dip toward year-end — a renewed marketing
   push could help sustain growth.
2. Technology has the highest profit margin; Furniture the lowest — recommend
   reviewing Furniture's discount strategy.
3. Central region leads in sales, while Canada and Caribbean show the lowest
   performance — indicating potential expansion opportunities.
4. Apple Smart Phones is the top-selling product — Technology dominates the
   top products list.
5. Consumer segment drives 51% of total sales — the key revenue contributor.
6. Copiers and Phones lead Technology revenue; Supplies and Fasteners
   contribute the least.
